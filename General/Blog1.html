Where To Stay In Singapore – Our Favorite Areas And Places

By Chris The Traveller

June 17, 2024

1. Marina Bay

Marina Bay is a top choice for tourists looking to experience the luxurious side of Singapore. It's home to the iconic Marina Bay Sands, a range of high-end shopping, and stunning views of the city skyline.

2. Orchard Road

If shopping is your priority, Orchard Road is the place to be. This bustling area is filled with shopping malls, restaurants, and hotels, making it a convenient and vibrant place to stay.

3. Chinatown

Chinatown offers a mix of traditional culture and modern attractions. You can explore heritage sites, enjoy delicious local food, and stay in boutique hotels that reflect the area's rich history.

4. Sentosa Island

For a more relaxed and fun-filled stay, Sentosa Island is ideal. It's known for its beaches, resorts, and attractions like Universal Studios Singapore and Adventure Cove Waterpark.

5. Bugis and Kampong Glam

These areas are great for those who want to experience Singapore's vibrant arts and culture scene. You can find trendy cafes, street art, and unique boutiques in this hip part of the city.
